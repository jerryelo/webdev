Hidden Gems

Annie's Canyon Trail

It is a local hiking trail that the visitor will have a very unique experience while passing through narrow slot canyon route to a wonderful amazing overlook point.The hike is short and classified as easy one. The length of the trail is 1.5miles and it will approximately take 38mins to complete it.

San Elijo Lagoon Ecological Reserve and Nature Center

Annie Canyon trail is also part of San Elijo Lagoon Nature Center.This center offers 7 miles of trails and eight trailheads around the Lagoon.Inside the center, there are various historical facts and interactive displays. Displays will give you an idea of what you will discover while walking around the lagoon.

La Jolla Cove

Small coastal inlet surrounded by sandstone bluffs that shelter the La Jolla Cove Beach. It is a famous place because sea lions, native plants and cormorants provide a pretty view. Also, the cove is very close to Village of La Jolla(downtown.
