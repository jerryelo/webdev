San Diego Zoo

The zoo offers different animals and species. Endangered species have reintroduced back into the wild. The location of the zoo is on hills therefore it requires full workout to complete the trip. It is strongly recommended to wear comfortable shoes. There are so many not-to-miss things to do in the zoo.

  • Experiences: Different animals in action experience, discovery cart tour, inside look tours
  • Activities: 4D Theater,Botanical Tours,Guided Bus Tour, Wildlife Presentations, Skyfari Aerial Tram,and Kangaroo Bus.
  • Dining: Restraurant, Casual and Quick Bites

Please visit the official San Diego Zoo Website for information or tickets.
